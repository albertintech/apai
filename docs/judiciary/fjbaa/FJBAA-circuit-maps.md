## Current vs. Proposed Circuit Arrangement

This comparison shows the population imbalance in the current 11-circuit system (2 of 11 in band, 4.79:1 ratio) against the FJBAA's proposed 15-circuit system (14 of 15 in band, 1.40:1 ratio). Each colored square represents approximately the same number of people — visual uniformity *is* population equity.
