A federal framework establishing an independent agency to verify and publicly publish factual, documentary records about candidates for federal office -- without regulating speech, imposing ideological tests, or restricting ballot access.

## Overview

The CVTA creates a permanent, independent Federal Candidate Verification Agency responsible for verifying objective facts about federal candidates: identity, citizenship, age, residency, education, employment, military service, court records, and financial disclosure consistency. A separate Federal Candidate Eligibility Panel within the federal judiciary holds exclusive jurisdiction over constitutional eligibility disputes.
